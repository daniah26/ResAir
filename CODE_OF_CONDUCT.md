
# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, the contributors and maintainers of **ResAir**, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, disability, ethnicity, religion, or level of experience.

We are committed to creating an inclusive, friendly, and collaborative environment for all participants.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming language.
- Being respectful.
- Providing constructive feedback.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Personal attacks or discriminatory comments.
- Harassment or intimidation of any kind.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent.
- Disrespectful or aggressive communication.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project lead directly via GitHub.

All complaints will be reviewed and handled fairly and confidentially.
